About Me

I am currently a third-year undergraduate at UC Berkeley studying Data Science with an emphasis in Human Cognition. I've always grown up interested in science and technolgoy, so I initially came into college looking to study biochemistry in hopes of eventually looking for life outside of our home planet in a field called Astrobiology. I was heavily inspired by an Astrophysics class I took with Alex Filippenko (who I grew up watching on TV), and decided to try out Astrophysics and Planetary Science as majors. Then, while doing a research experience the summer after my freshman year while also taking an introductory Data Science class, I became deeply interested in what data and computational techniques had to offer us. And so, I landed upon Berkeley's new Data Science major, where I became excited about all the possible techniques and applications of machine intelligence.

I decided to try and immerse myself into as much as I could in the fields of computer science and statistics. I've become filled with an intrinsic desire to learn because I finally realize that I'm studying so that I can be a part of this collective effort towards a better and more knowledgeable society. And this excites me so much.There is so much technology being developed, and we need to make an effort so that it is used to make a positive change in the world.

I am interested in a career where I can utilize Artificial Intelligence to explore our world and the worlds around us so that we can make a positive impact. Specifically, I have become interested in studying Deep Neural Networks. I have interests in applying AI to space science, earth science, physics, robotics, and so much more.
